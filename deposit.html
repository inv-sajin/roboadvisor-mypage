Transfers from a Securities Account
You can deposit funds into your securities account and then transfer them to xxx for operations.
STEP 1 Deposit into the Securities Account
[1] or [2] - Choose one method to deposit into your securities account.
1. Online Transfer
If you have applied for and use the internet services of the designated financial institution, you can make transfers online.
Click below to see the list of available financial institutions.
2. Bank Transfer
Since a designated deposit account has been assigned to you, you can also transfer funds via bank transfer within Japan.
Note: Transfer fees set by the financial institution you use will apply. Refunds may occur for any overpaid fees depending on the financial institution or process.
After login, navigate to "Deposits (Transfers)" to proceed.
STEP 2 Transfer Funds from the Securities Account to xxx
Go to the "xxx Account Transfer - Input Instructions" page to specify the amount to transfer into xxx.
  • Check that the transferable amount is displayed.
  • The timing for the funds to reflect may vary based on the financial institution after completing STEP 1.
After logging in, you will be redirected to the "xxx Account Transfer - Input Instructions" page.
STEP 3 Deposit Completed
Deposits will be reflected in your transaction history on xxx's My Page between 17:00-23:00 on business days.
